03 : My Story

Background

I was born born and raised in Nairobi and later moved to Ukunda, Kwale. I’ve always been drawn to the intersection of technology and human connection. My journey began with a curiosity for how systems work and a desire to make life more efficient and accessible through digital solutions. That curiosity led me to pursue a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology, where I immersed myself in programming, system administration, and cybersecurity.

Career

Over time, I’ve developed a versatile skill set that combines technical expertise with practical problem-solving. My experience includes system administration, Python programming, and user support, each role sharpening my ability to troubleshoot, optimize, and innovate. I’ve worked in both on-site and remote settings, adapting quickly to new technologies and workflows. From configuring devices and maintaining secure networks to designing digital content and streamlining operations, I’ve consistently achieved results that improve efficiency and user experience.

Passion & Drive

What motivates me is the belief that technology should empower people rather than complicate their lives. I’m passionate about creating digital experiences that are intuitive, secure, and impactful. I find purpose in making technology feel human. I thrive in dynamic environments where learning is continuous, and I’m driven by a desire to innovate, collaborate, and grow. My goal is to contribute to projects that not only solve problems but also inspire progress because, for me, every line of code is a step toward a better-connected world.
